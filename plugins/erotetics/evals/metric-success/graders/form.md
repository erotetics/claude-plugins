Score artifact FORM — the product claim is a consistent decision artifact, not
an essay. One point per criterion (0–6), normalised to 0–1:

1. Canonical four-section skeleton: SURFACE / EXAMINE / CHALLENGE / REFRAME.
2. EXAMINE rendered as a table with a load-bearing column AND an
   evidence-status column (verified / merely assumed / contradicted).
3. Explicitly names decorative / safe-to-ignore assumptions as out of scope.
4. Every CHALLENGE is priced with a cheapest settling test.
5. Ends with exactly one clearly marked closing question
   ("The question to settle: …").
6. Reads as a decision document (skimmable, ≤ ~900 words), not open prose.
